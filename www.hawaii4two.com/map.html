Our location is the ideal home base for exploring the island!

We're located about 30 minutes south of Kailua-Kona, overlooking the City of Refuge National Historic Park on the sunny, Kona Coast of Hawaii. Although situated in a secluded area, we are only about 10-15 minutes away from sandy beaches, the islands best snorkeling, scuba diving, dolphins, turtles, kayaking, biking, fishing, hiking, and a myriad of other island activities, shopping and restaurants.
