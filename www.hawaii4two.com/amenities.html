Our Vacation Cottages come loaded with amenities!

At check-in:

• Generous fruit-bowl filled with pineapple, bananas, papayas and other tropical fruits from the plantation
• Our own Bwana Bob's Estate Grown 100% Kona coffee, roasted to order,available for sale when you leave or on the Internet.

If you should run out of fruit we encourage you to help yourself to more from the trees on the plantation. At various times you’ll find “apple” bananas, breadfruit, cheremoya, fig, guava, grapefruit, lemon, longan, loquat, lychee, mangoes, oranges, regular and “strawberry” papaya, passionfruit (lilikoi), persimmon, pineapples, sapote and of course, all the macadamia nuts you can pick and eat!

Both our Cottages are designated non-smoking (outdoors is fine) and share the common lawn areas. Laundry facilities are available on the premises, in our house.

Inside:

• Kitchen equipped with sink & garbage disposal, built-in 2 burner cooktop, full size refrigerator, microwave, toaster, blender, coffeemaker, coffee grinder, citrus juicer and quality dishes, pots, pans, and cutlery. The Banana Bungalow has a full size range/oven and a dishwasher.
• Paper and cleaning goods, basic spices and condiments such as olive oil, vinegar, soy sauce, tobasco, honey, sugar, creamer, mayonnaise, mustard, and ketchup.

• Queen bed with down comforter, luxury linens and towels (all 100% cotton).
• Complimentary kimonos to wear during your stay.

• Premium-channel satellite TV & Music, VCR & DVD players...bring your own cd's & dvd's!
Stereo with CD, cassette and radio (bring your own CD’s or tapes)

• free wireless internet for the Jungle Cottage and Banana Bungalow guests on our lanai.
• Alarm clock, hair dryer, iron and ironing board.
• Ceiling fans

Outside:

• Teak “steamer” chaise-lounges for relaxing on the lawns (2 per Cottage).
• Both our cottages have their own BBQ grills.
• Beach chairs and umbrella, beach towels, snorkels, fins and masks, portable ice cooler & jug, blue ice and even boogie boards!
